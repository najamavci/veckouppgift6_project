Easy Pancakes

This is a simple pancake recipe that is great for breakfast or a quick dessert. It uses a few basic ingredients and is easy for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 500 ml milk
  • 2.5 dl flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Butter for frying

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the batter

Mix the flour and half of the milk in a bowl until smooth.

Add the rest of the milk, eggs, and salt. Mix well.

Step 2: Fry the pancakes

Heat a frying pan and melt a little butter.

Pour in a small amount of batter and fry each pancake until golden on both sides.

Step 3: Serve

Serve with jam, berries, sugar, or whipped cream.
